About Us

We Eagle (SZ) Industrial Ltd. are a professional manufacturer and exporter with self-support importing & exporting license. We specialize in all kinds of liquid-filled promotion gifts and premium products.

With 20 years of OEM experience, we can meet your product customization needs based on the technical drawings. We offer customization options such as floaters, liquid color, inside glitter, logo printing, and packaging to meet your needs.
